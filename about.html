About Me

I’m Sara, I’m a messy perfectionist with a passion for Art. It soothes me whenever I pick up a pencil or brush, with music playing on the background and a scented candle.
I was born in Lisbon, Portugal and decided to move to England when I was 19 years old. I now live in the gorgeous county of Derbyshire with my Fiancé, who is very supportive of my work.
My mother used to draw my school notebooks’ covers and my father used to paint landscapes and portraits. I admired their work and ended up learning from them. In 2009, I started drawing for my friends and family; watching their reaction to the drawings I made for them always motivated me to continue.

I attended the Visual Arts course at school and right after I took a Conservation and Restoration Assistant Apprenticeship, which concluded in 2015. This route in Education helped me acquire new art techniques, I learned how to work with different mediums and overall, I got to discover myself within the world of Art.

This is what I love to do and hopefully others will find joy in my creations.
